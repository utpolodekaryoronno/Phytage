Why Am I Suffering From Tinnitus?

The scientific and medical communities report a number of different things that can trigger tinnitus symptoms. You might recognize your trigger below, and if not don’t worry - it’s not at all a complete lits.

Loud noise exposure

Such as from concerts, machinery, or loud music can be a contributing factor.

Age related

As people get older, they can develop tinnitus due to changes in the ear.

Medications

Some medicines have tinnitus as a side effect for some people without affecting others.

Head or neck injuries

Historical injuries can affect the inner ear, hearing nerves, or brain functions linked to hearing.

Inner ear muscle spasms

The tube that connects the middle ear to the back of the nose can sometimes malfunction

Other medical conditions

Thyroid problems, Lyme disease, fibromyalgia, and other conditions can also lead to tinnitus
